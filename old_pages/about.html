Our story

An Evolution of interactive sports

Control Protocol is the next evolution of Fan Controlled Football (FCF), a groundbreaking sports league where fans call the shots, from plays to team decisions. Originating from a concept that combined sports, fan engagement, and technology, FCF brought an unprecedented level of interaction to the game of football. The league saw the Wild Aces, co-owned by NFL star Marshawn Lynch, claim victory in a historic first season, setting the stage for what sports could become in the digital age.

As we embarked on the second season, the league expanded, welcoming new teams and enhancing the viewer experience with strategic partnerships, including a deal with NBCUniversal for broadcast rights. This season not only featured the construction of a bespoke arena in Atlanta, Georgia but also showcased the dynamic championship win of the Zappers, a team with the backing of notable personalities such as Bob Menery, Johnny Manziel, and Terrel Owens (TO).

Our Mission

The future of Sports is Interactive

At Control Protocol, our mission is to revolutionize the sports industry by empowering fans and players alike through blockchain technology. We believe in creating a transparent, engaging, and interactive sports ecosystem where fans don't just watch but actively participate and shape the game's future. Our commitment to innovation, community, and empowerment drives us to build a platform that bridges the digital and physical worlds of sports, making every game more dynamic and interactive.

By fostering a decentralized and fan-driven sports ecosystem, we're not just changing how games are played and experienced; we're building a community where everyone has a voice. Our vision for the future of sports is interactive, inclusive, and infinitely exciting. Welcome to Control Protocol, where the future of sports is not just watched but actively shaped by its community.
