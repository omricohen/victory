Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Victory Chain Node?

A Victory Chain Node is a validator that helps secure and maintain the network by processing transactions and participating in consensus.

How are rewards calculated?

Rewards are calculated based on your node type, stake amount, and network participation. Higher tier nodes receive priority in block validation and higher APY.

Can I upgrade my node later?

Yes, you can upgrade your node by staking additional VICTRY tokens and meeting the higher tier requirements.

What happens if my node goes offline?

Nodes that go offline may face reduced rewards or penalties. Our monitoring system will alert you of any issues to maintain optimal performance.
