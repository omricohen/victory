Frequently Asked Questions

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected using a verifiable random function (VRF) through Chainlink's oracle network, ensuring completely fair and transparent selection. Your chances of winning are proportional to your stake amount.

When can I withdraw my tokens?

You can withdraw your staked tokens at any time. There are no lockup periods or penalties for withdrawal. Your tokens remain fully under your control.

What's the minimum stake amount?

The minimum stake amount is 100 VICTRY tokens. This ensures fair participation while maintaining efficient protocol operations.

How often are prizes distributed?

Prize drawings occur daily at 00:00 UTC. Traditional staking rewards are distributed automatically every 24 hours to your staking wallet.

Can I participate in both staking methods?

Yes, but each individual token can only be staked in one vault type at a time. You can split your tokens between the two vaults, staking some for steady yields and others for prize opportunities. You can also change a token's staking type at any time.

What happens if I win a prize?

Prize winnings are automatically transferred to your wallet within 24 hours of winning. For non-token prizes (like VIP experiences or node licenses), our team will contact you directly through your registered email to arrange delivery.

Are my staked tokens safe?

Absolutely. The Victory Vault smart contracts have been audited by leading security firms and use industry-standard security practices. Your tokens remain non-custodial, meaning you maintain full control of your assets at all times.

How are the prize pools funded?

All tokens in the Prize Vault are automatically staked in the traditional staking pool. Instead of distributing the yields to individual stakers, these rewards are collected into a single prize pool. This pool is then used for the daily prize drawings, allowing for larger potential winnings than individual staking rewards.

What's the difference between Traditional and Prize Vault staking?

In Traditional staking, you receive your yield rewards directly as they are generated. In the Prize Vault, your tokens still earn the same yield, but instead of receiving small daily distributions, all yields go into a shared prize pool. This pool is then awarded to lucky winners through daily drawings, offering the chance to win much larger amounts than individual staking rewards.

How do I change my staking type?

You can change a token's staking type by first withdrawing it from its current vault, then re-staking it in your preferred vault type. This process can be done at any time with no penalties or waiting periods.
