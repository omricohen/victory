Frequently asked questions

Whether you're curious about the functionalities of Victory Chain, its impact on fan experiences, or how to get involved, this section is designed to guide and inform you.
  • What is SportsFi?
    SportsFi combines sports and finance through blockchain technology, enabling interactive fan experiences, real-time sports analytics, and fan-driven governance.
  • How does Victory Chain work?
    Victory Chain operates as a Layer-2 blockchain solution focused on sports, supporting real-time stats, NFTs, and DAOs for a comprehensive sports digital ecosystem.
  • What are NFTs, and how are they used in sports?
    NFTs, or Non-Fungible Tokens, represent unique digital collectibles or moments in sports on the blockchain, allowing fans to own, trade, and collect digital sports memorabilia.
  • What are fan-run DAOs?
    Fan-run DAOs are decentralized autonomous organizations that give sports enthusiasts governance rights, allowing them to vote on team decisions, developments, and strategies.
  • How can developers build on Victory Chain?
    Developers can utilize Victory Chain's comprehensive APIs and EVM compatibility to create decentralized apps, analytics tools, and immersive fan experiences.
  • Is Victory Chain secure?
    Yes, Victory Chain leverages blockchain technology to ensure security and transparency, with advanced mechanisms for data integrity and transaction verification.
